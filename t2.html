Pazani has been with SANNE Mauritius for over 10 years where he heads a closed-end fund unit responsible for handling the specific needs of private equity investors. In addition to his private equity specialism, he has also acquired in-depth knowledge and expertise of the global business industry with hands-on exposure in accounting, tax, corporate structuring and set-ups, AML, legal and secretarial matters. Pazani is a Fellow of the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA) UK and holds a Bachelor of Science in Management with Finance with first class distinction from the University of Mauritius.
